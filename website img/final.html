Here is my final project!

Lost in the Woods

href= "https://youtu.be/-Kmo1BKKQBk"

My final project is a choose-your-own adventure mini game. You're stuck in the woods and
have a choice between two paths to take. One path will take you to a village where you can get help
while the other leads to a pack of hungry wolves. Click on the path you want to take.
